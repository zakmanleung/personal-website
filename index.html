About

I am an incoming PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Arizona, starting August 2026. I completed my Master of Science in Computer Science at the University of Bristol with Distinction in All Courses, and my Bachelor of Engineering in Materials Science and Engineering (with a minor in Japanese) at Central South University, graduating as an Outstanding Graduate with a WAM of 83%.

My research interests lie in Usability, with a focus on Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Data Visualization. I am particularly interested in designing intuitive interfaces and visual analytics tools that enhance user experience and decision-making. My previous work includes building interactive data dashboards for real-time monitoring and developing user-centered analytics platforms for cross-functional teams.

Previously, I worked as a Senior Data Analyst & MAM Product Manager at JCDecaux Group, and as a Manager of Data Department at Esquel Group. I have extensive experience in leading cross-functional teams and translating business questions into model-driven research.
